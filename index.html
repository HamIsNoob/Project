Welcome

What you can expect from our programs

The focus of “Think Global” is to offer a once
in a lifetime opportunity to New Zealand students
to travel across the global and experience what
other unique countries have to offer. Think Global
would offer exchange programs for university students
to travel to less developed countries to learn about
their culture, religion and way of living. This would
reflect Think Global’s vision of respect, acceptance
and appreciation as it would give students a hands-on
first-person perspective of how different countries run.

















About Us

Think Global is a non-profit organisation that strives to change lives through travelling.
We aim to take students across the world to experience life from a different country. Think
Global’s team is filled with experienced members that has been through a multitude of different
countries and lifestyles, they are able to aid and guide you one hundred percent of the way.
